Our Mission

Navjeevan is a School at Neemuch & Rampura (Madhya Pradesh) built with the purpose of serving children with learning difficulties and makes their life easy.

We envisage and endeavor to change the lives of children with special needs by teaching them the skills they need to deal with the world and feel good and confident about them. Each child is different and needs individual approach to learn and to move forward, and we aspire to cater to the needs of each individualâ€™s learning.

Our Vision

Our Goal is to develop a School to promote an evidence based education system where children with special needs study in classrooms where their psychological needs are taken care of and there is no scope for disillusionment, disengagement or depression.

Education must be an equal opportunity for everyone!

Every child has all the rights to be educated and to live their life to the fullest. Unfortunately, children with learning difficulties struggle at a mainstream school. Parents and even educators do not understand the plight of the child due to lack of awareness.

Navjeevan School is the place for children with special need for learning difficulties and academic challenges. Our special educated teachers focus on each child and help in their difficulty to solve problem.
